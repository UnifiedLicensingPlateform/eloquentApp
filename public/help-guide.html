Troubleshooting & FAQ
‚ùì
Speech Recognition Not Working?
Solution: Make sure you're using Chrome, Edge, or Safari. Check that microphone permissions are enabled for the website. Firefox is not currently supported.
‚ùì
Text Not Being Recognized?
Solution: Speak clearly and at a normal pace. Ensure you're in a quiet environment with minimal background noise. Check your microphone settings.
‚ùì
AI Improvements Not Working?
Solution: This feature requires a Gemini API key. Pro users get this automatically. Free users can add their own API key in settings.
‚ùì
Reached Monthly Limit?
Solution: Free users have monthly limits. Upgrade to Pro for unlimited access, or wait until next month for your limits to reset.
